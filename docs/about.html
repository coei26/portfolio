my vision board

things that inspire me as a designer.

ANNIE LEIBOVITZ PHOTOGRAPHY

Empathy is essential when designing for people. I can think of no greater example of someone who has such an eye for the world, someone who can understand & capture emotion so artfully like Annie Leibovitz. Her portraits her masterful, staging purposeful, and her photographs are remembered, celebrated, and appeal to people of all walks of life.

DON NORMAN

The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman is my ultimate guide for design. It has not only taught me how to think as a designer, but also how to be a human-centered designer. In this UX Podcast, Don Norman discusses design thinking, living with complexity, and the future of design in the midst of AI and VR developments.

PUDDING.COOL

Ever heard of The Pudding? They're creators of visual essays leveraging data to educate and share news. Some of my favorite essays are Women in Headlines, which looks at how women are represented in the news, The Naked Truth, and one that looks at algorithms and provides insights on how to make writing more accessible, called What Makes Writing More Readable?
